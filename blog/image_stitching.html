Image stitching for arbitrary camera motion

Panorama is an image obtained from stitching multiple images obtained from horizontal motion of camera. This projects aims to stitch multiple images obtained from arbitrary motion of camera. For the purpose of demonstration, a set of 7 images of a hostel room were snapped by a mobile camera. The images were scaled down to reduce processing time.
