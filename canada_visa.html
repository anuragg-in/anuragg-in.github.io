Timeline for Canada tourist visa application
I applied for a Canada visitor visa under the CAN+ program from New York, USA. Here's the timeline for my application process.
12-Aug-2023: Submitted application for a Canada visitor visa.
16-Aug-2023: Biometrics request from the IRCC office.
21-Aug-2023: Biometrics enrollment at VFS Mumbai, India.
29-Aug-2023: Application approved. Passport submission request from IRCC office.
11-Sep-2023: Registered for two-way courier service from Delhi.
13-Sep-2023: Passport picked up by Bluedart.
14-Sep-2023: Passport received by VFS Mumbai, India. Passport dispatched to VFS Bangalore, India.
15-Sep-2023: Passport received by VFS Bangalore, India. Passport dispatched to the IRCC office, Bangalore, India. Passport received by the IRCC office, Bangalore, India.
19-Sep-2023: Passport and visa counterfoil dispatched by the IRCC office, Bangalore, India. Application portal updated with visa details. Passport arrived at VFS Bangalore, India. Passport dispatched by VFS Bangalore, India.
20-Sep-2023: Passport delivered.
Remarks
  • Even though I applied from USA, I had no issues with biometrics and passport submission in India.
  • I did not opt for the premium lounge service while biometrics enrollment at the VFS centre and the entire process did not take more than half an hour.
  • I did not opt for the SMS notification service. VFS sent me numerous notifications via e-mails on the status of my application.
  • The delay between the passport request and the passport submission was due to my travel plans. Moreover, there was an extended weekend holiday in IRCC office, Bangalore, India from 16-Sep-2023 to 18-Sep-2023 due to Ganesh Chaturthi.
